Toronto

Toronto is the capital of the province of Ontario and is a major Canadian city along Lake Ontario’s northwestern shore. It's a dynamic metropolis with a core of soaring skyscrapers.

Toronto Tourist Attractions

  • CN Tower
  • Royal Ontario Museum
  • Niagara Falls
  • University of Toronto
  • Toronto Islands

Toronto Fun Facts

  1. Toronto is the largest city in Canada.
  2. Toronto is home to North America's only castle.
  3. Approximately half of Toronto's population was born outside of Canada.
  4. The Toronto Islands is the largest urban car-free community in North America.
  5. Toronto has a total of 9,520 streets.
